A Note from Home

Welcome to our live scrapbook for the 2026 National High School Model United Nations conference!

As parents and supporters, we know firsthand just how much effort, intelligence, and sheer willpower went into preparing for this trip. We created this page to stay connected and celebrate our incredible team as they take on New York City and the UN Headquarters.

Here, you'll find live photo updates, daily briefings, and a behind-the-scenes look at our delegates in action. A massive thank you to the teachers who made this expedition possible and who are keeping everything running smoothly on the ground.

To our delegates: Have an amazing time, debate fiercely, and make some unforgettable memories. We can't wait to see the world through your eyes this week.
